Discover the Arts
Zambales

San Antonio

Is a Second Class Municipality becoming the crown jewel of tourism in Zambales and Region III.

Casa San Miguel

It serves as a hub for arts education and community development, featuring a music school, art residency programs, and a museum.

Zambulat Fetival

It features live concerts from local bands, art exhibits, a trade fair of Zambales-based products, and various activities like kite making and mural painting.

Zaniel Mariano

Mariano, who hails from San Antonio, Zambales, is a self taught artist and began his career in painting at the age of 16.

Elmer Borlongan

Elmer Borlongan is a contemporary Philippine painter. He is one of the CCP Thirteen Artists Awardees. He and his artist-wife, Plet C. Bolipata, live in San Antonio, Zambales.

Pundaquit Festival

The Pundaquit Festival is an annual community arts program by CASA San Miguel in San Antonio, Zambales, that fosters local arts through residencies, performances, workshops, and exhibits.

Alfonso Coke Bolipata

Alfonso "Coke" Bolipataa is a highly acclaimed Filipino violinist. He is also a teacher, writer, administrator, and founder of Casa San Miguel in San Antonio, Zambales.
Zambales

San Marcelino

Vibrant community arts celebrating the town's agricultural pride.

Singkamas Fesstival

The Singkamas Festival is a week-long celebration in San Marcelino, Zambales, that honors the region's abundant harvest of singkamas (jicama).

Ricarte Ico

"Ricarte Ico" is one of the contemporary artist known for his Neo-Cubism art, who sometimes goes by the name "RicARTE".

Weaving & Basketry

Traditional utility and decorative items crafted from bamboo and rattan. Weaving is the general art of intertwining threads to make fabric, while basketry is the more specific craft of weaving pliable materials like grasses, reeds, or twigs to create three-dimensional objects like baskets, mats, and even furniture.
Zambales

Castillejos

The artistic legacy of leadership and public service preserved through historical artifacts.

Ramon Magsaysay Ancestral House

The Ramon Magsaysay Ancestral House is a museum and ancestral home in Castillejos, Zambales, that served as the childhood home of the late Philippine President Ramon Magsaysay.

Mark Ronald Sioson

Born in the modest town of Castillejos, located in the heart of Zambales Province, he is a self-taught artist who fuses his works with the concept of realism.

Suman Ibos Festival

The Suman Ibos Festival is a celebration in honor of the traditional Filipino delicacy, suman sa ibos, a sticky rice cake wrapped in buli leaves.

Bamboo Art

Bamboo art is the practice of creating objects and artworks from the versatile and abundant plant, bamboo.

Experience the Fusion of Culture and Nature

From the vibrant Singkamas harvest to the classical symphonies of Casa San Miguel, Zambales invites you to witness art in every form.
